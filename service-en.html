Services

Development and management of investment and construction projects. Comprehensive project support from site analysis to concept formation and implementation strategy. We assess land potential, risks, infrastructure and create the foundation for effective development.
2. Design & Engineering →
  • architectural design
  • engineering solutions
  • structural systems

Architecture and engineering solutions. Development of architectural concepts and engineering systems taking into account the climate, terrain and construction features of Samui. Focus on functionality, durability and integration with the natural environment.

Construction management. Full management of the construction process: coordination of contractors, control of deadlines, budget and quality of work. Ensuring transparency and technical discipline at all stages of implementation.

Technical control and expertise of properties. Independent assessment of construction solutions and quality of work. Inspection of properties before purchase or start of construction: structure, engineering, waterproofing, risks.

  • turnkey construction
  • renovation and reconstruction
  • project handover
Turnkey construction and implementation. Full cycle of project implementation — from construction work to final delivery. Renovation, reconstruction and construction of ready-made objects with quality control at every stage.
