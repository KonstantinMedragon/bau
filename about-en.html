About company

My name is Dmitry, I am the founder of BAU co. But first and foremost — I am a civil engineer.

In 1997, as a first-year student at the Civil Engineering Institute of St. Petersburg Polytechnic University (SPbPU), in one of the old lecture halls, I first heard Vitruvius' Latin triad, which is over two thousand years old: "Firmitas. Utilitas. Venustas" — Strength. Utility. Beauty.

Back then, they were just beautiful words on the blackboard. But over 30 years of real construction practice, going from harsh industrial facilities to private development, I realized: this is not just a formula of architecture.

Every man has an innate desire to create — to turn chaos into order, to give form to ideas, and to lay a foundation that he will not be ashamed of before his sons. A builder is a creator who, like a father, protects his project and bears personal, unconditional responsibility for it.

Having moved to Samui, I saw how often people build for the sake of a "beautiful picture," forgetting the essence. That is why at BAU co. I returned to that very golden standard I adopted for myself back in my student days:

✦ FIRMITAS (Strength): This is iron engineering precision and strict technical supervision. The creation must be flawless from the inside; in a tropical climate, compromises with quality are unacceptable.
✦ UTILITAS (Utility): Honest communication, transparent estimates, and respect for your budget. The home should serve the person, bringing peace, not headaches.
✦ VENUSTAS (Beauty): Competent, professional implementation. We take a bold visual concept and turn it into impeccable, durable aesthetics.

I am personally involved in every stage — from the first drawings to final renovation. The real magic begins where the drawing meets the land of Samui. My signature on the project is my personal responsibility to you, to my reputation, and to that first-year student from 1997.

We do not just build walls. We create your world — reliable and safe.
