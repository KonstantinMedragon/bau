About company

My name is Dmitry, founder of BAU co. But first and foremost — I am a civil engineer.

In 1997, as a student at the Faculty of Civil Engineering of St. Petersburg Polytechnic University, in one of the old lecture halls, I first heard the Latin Triad of Vitruvius — the fundamental principle of architecture, formulated by the ancient Roman architect and engineer Marcus Vitruvius Pollio in the 1st century BC.

"Firmitas. Utilitas. Venustas" — Strength. Utility. Beauty.

Over 30 years of real construction practice, going from harsh industrial facilities to private development, I realized: this is not just a formula of architecture.

Every man has an innate desire to create — to turn chaos into order, to give form to ideas, and to lay a foundation that he will not be ashamed of before his children. A builder is a Creator who, like a father, protects his project and bears personal responsibility for it.

Having moved to Thailand, I saw how often people build for the sake of a "beautiful picture," forgetting the essence. That is why at BAU co. I returned to the golden standard I adopted for myself back in my student days:

✦ Strength (lat. Firmitas) — reliability and durability. The structure must be stable, reliably protect from external influences and preserve its construction over time.
✦ Utility (lat. Utilitas) — practicality and functionality. The building must be perfectly suited for its purpose, comfortable and safe.
✦ Beauty (lat. Venustas) — aesthetic appeal. The building should please the eye, have harmonious proportions and delight the viewer with its appearance.

I am personally involved in every stage — from geodetic surveys to commissioning. The real magic begins where the drawing meets the ground. My signature on the contract is my personal responsibility to you and my reputation.

We don't just build — we create your Strong — Useful — Beautiful and Ergonomic space!

Marcus Vitruvius Pollio
